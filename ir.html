The approach

Many engines. One smooth ride.

Most strategies live or die on a single bet. We combine several low-correlation return sources so that when one is quiet, another carries — keeping the equity curve calmer through bull, bear, and chop.

⚖️

Market-neutral core

A dollar-neutral long/short book: long quality assets, short structurally weak, high-inflation tokens. Low directional exposure to the overall market.

🧭

Uncorrelated overlays

A trend-following sleeve and a gold (PAXG) sleeve add return streams that move independently of the core — true diversification, not duplication.

🛡️

Risk first

Volatility targeting, regime-aware de-risking, position limits, and conservative leverage. The goal is a shallow drawdown profile, not a lottery ticket.

Under the hood

Three independent engines

01

Core Long/Short (dollar-neutral)

Permanent conviction longs (BTC, HYPE) plus dynamically-selected layer-1s screened on on-chain TVL growth. Against them, a broad, diversified short book of high-inflation tokens in sustained downtrends. Balanced long vs short to neutralise market beta.

Primary engine · auto-rebalanced
02

Trend Following

A small directional sleeve that rides only the strongest, confirmed trends (momentum + trend-strength filtered), sized in tiers. Near-zero correlation to the core — it captures the breakouts the neutral book cannot.

Diversifier · reviewed every 2h
03

Gold (PAXG) Sleeve

Holds tokenized gold only while gold is in a confirmed uptrend, otherwise flat. An asset essentially uncorrelated with crypto — a calm, low-volatility ballast for the portfolio.

Ballast · trend-gated
Risk management

Drawdown discipline, by design

Returns are a by-product of surviving. The system continuously dials risk up and down rather than chasing every move.

Volatility targeting

Exposure scales down automatically when markets get turbulent, and back up when they calm — keeping realized risk roughly constant.

Regime de-risking

When altcoins broadly outperform (the classic short-squeeze regime), the short book is automatically reduced to avoid being caught offside.

Broad diversification

Dozens of small short positions instead of a few large ones — single-name shocks barely move the portfolio.

No liquidation by design

Conservative, dollar-neutral leverage keeps the account far from any liquidation threshold even in violent markets.
